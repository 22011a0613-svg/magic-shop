Our story

Why Magic Shop exists

It started with a visit to a government emporium — and a heartbreaking realization that India's most beautiful crafts were quietly disappearing.

🏛️
The moment it began

A visit that changed everything

Walking through a government emporium, surrounded by breathtaking handlooms and handicrafts, we learned something that broke our hearts — demand for these pieces kept declining year after year. Artisans were getting poorer. Their children were leaving for cities. Entire craft traditions were dying with their last practitioners.

These weren't just beautiful objects. They were living languages — visual stories passed down through generations. And they were going silent.

💡
The idea

What if a shop could feel like a magic place?

The name Magic Shop comes from the idea of a comforting place — somewhere that feels emotional, meaningful, and safe. Like the BTS song that inspired it, we wanted to create a space where people don't just buy things, they feel something.

We believe the reason crafts are declining isn't that people don't care — it's that they've never truly been introduced to them. Once you know Rajamma's story, you don't just see a saree. You see forty years of devotion woven into silk.

🌱
The mission

Commerce as a force for cultural preservation

Magic Shop is built on a simple belief: if we can make these crafts economically viable, future generations of artisans will choose to continue them. Not out of obligation — but because it's a good life.

We start in Telangana, a state rich with GI-tagged crafts and master artisans whose work deserves global recognition. From here, we plan to expand across India — and eventually, the world.

Our roadmap

Where we're headed

We're just getting started. Here's the journey we've planned.

2025

Launch in Telangana

Start with 10–15 artisans across 5 craft traditions. Launch online store and waitlist. Build our storytelling content library.

2026

Expand across India

Onboard artisans from Rajasthan, West Bengal, Tamil Nadu and more. Launch B2B corporate gifting. Begin tourism partnerships.

2027

First experience centers

Open physical experience centers in high-footfall tourist areas. Run live craft demonstrations and cultural events.

2028+

Go global

Export to international markets. Partner with global lifestyle brands. Put Indian craft on the world's cultural map.

What we stand for

Our values

Everything we build, every decision we make, comes back to these four things.

🤝
Artisan first
Every business decision is measured against one question: does this make things better for the artisan?
📖
Story over product
We sell stories that happen to come with beautiful objects — not objects that happen to have a story attached.
🌿
Sustainable always
Eco-friendly packaging, natural materials, fair wages. Sustainability isn't a feature — it's the foundation.
🔍
Radical transparency
We show you exactly who made your product, what they were paid, and how the craft was done. No mystery, no middlemen.
